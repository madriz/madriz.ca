Background

The short version

I'm a mechanical engineer by training, with a Master of Engineering Management from the University of Colorado at Boulder and an MBA from York University's Schulich School of Business. I also completed the Program on Negotiation and Leadership at Harvard.

My career has moved between building my own companies and working inside large ones. I've co-founded four ventures with two exits, and held senior roles at TELUS and Bell Canada, among others. Earlier I worked in with heavy machinery at Husky Injection Moulding and Fiat Avio (now Siemens), Ingersoll Dresser Pumps, as well as with a Honeywell & Allen Bradley system integrator for the Oil & Gas sector.

More recently I've focused on advisory work -- supporting venture-backed companies across AI, fintech, and media with strategy, product development, and commercial scaling.

Fluent in English, Spanish, and Italian. Two US patent applications, one issued. Based in Toronto.
